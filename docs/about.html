Our Story

Started Small.
Built to Last.

SAW Incorporation was founded in 2018 by three engineers who kept seeing the same pattern: businesses were being sold bloated, overpriced digital work that looked great in a pitch deck but failed in production.

We started with a different premise — that the best agency work is indistinguishable from in-house work. That means being opinionated, being accountable, and staying until the job is truly done.

Today we serve clients across four continents, from seed-stage startups to Fortune 500 subsidiaries. The same principles that got us here still govern every project we take on.

What We Stand For

Values That Drive Everything

These aren't wall-poster platitudes. They're the principles we use to make decisions every single day.

Outcomes Over Output

We measure success by what our clients achieve — not by deliverables shipped. If a project isn't moving your key metrics, we say so and course-correct.

Radical Transparency

Weekly async updates, shared roadmaps, open staging environments. No surprises at delivery. You're never wondering what's happening on your project.

Speed Without Shortcuts

We move fast by building right the first time. Clean architecture, tested code, and documentation mean future iterations cost a fraction of what they should.

Deep Collaboration

We embed with your team — attending standups, joining Slack, reading your strategy docs. The best work happens when the line between agency and client blurs.

Craft at Every Level

From API architecture to micro-interactions, we sweat the details others skip. Our code is reviewed. Our designs are pressure-tested. Our copy is intentional.

Long-Term Thinking

We build for the next three years, not the next sprint. Technology choices, design systems, and infrastructure are all made with scale and maintainability in mind.
